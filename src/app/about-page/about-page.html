Established in 1980

Buddhinee Jewellers (Private) Limited is a subsidiary of Rovelco Holdings (Private) Limited, which is also the parent company of New Lanka Gold House, Badulla, Sri Lanka, a fine jewellery dealer, which has been in the industry for over 40 years since 1980. The new retail showrooms have been rebranded as Buddhinee Jewellers since 2012, and the latest addition to the retail arm is in Kiribathgoda, Sri Lanka.

We are pioneers in handcrafted fine jewellery, with a strong force of experienced and talented craftsmen, with the capability to cater to any sort of jewellery requirement.

The attention to detail we put into our products is an indication of the passion we have for this art.

-The Management of Buddhinee jewellers
